About ABC community

ABC is a Community of the unique hand-drawn characters on the Ethereum Blockchain. Each circle member is DIFFERENT from one another; that's what UNIFIES them.

Every week one unique character will be released throughout the year. Each character will have its own 26 versions. These versions are differentiated by the alphabet letter they'll be given.
